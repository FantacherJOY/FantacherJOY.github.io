Fantacher Islam

PhD Candidate · Systems & Industrial Engineering
University of Arizona

I am a doctoral researcher in the Health Systems Engineering & Informatics Laboratory at the University of Arizona, advised by Dr. Vignesh Subbian. My work applies causal inference, clinical machine learning, and biomedical ontologies to real-world health data — including MIMIC-IV, the NIH RECOVER Cohort, and the All of Us Research Program — to improve clinical decision support and health outcomes.

§ 01

Research interests

Broadly, I am interested in causal inference and representation learning for longitudinal health data, with a particular focus on g-methods and neural sequence models for treatment-effect estimation. I care about methods that are reproducible, interpretable, and honest about uncertainty.
